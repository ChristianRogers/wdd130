About

Most game reviews you find online are either done by paid professionals from a company or online personalities, often paid to review content or given the game for free. These reviewers have different values and standards from the average gamer. That’s where I come in: a regular gamer reviewing games. I will tell you what you want to know, and if a game is worth your hard-earned money.

Instead of expensive OLED TVs and high-end gaming monitors that other reviewers use, I play on the same budget 4K and 1080p screens that many American families own. I also use the same consoles and modest PC hardware for my reviews, instead of thousand-dollar parts.
